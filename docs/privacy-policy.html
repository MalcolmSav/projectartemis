Artemis Privacy Policy

she who watches

Effective date: 12 July 2026 · Applies to the Artemis mobile app (iOS & Android) by Embel Studio.

What Artemis is

Artemis is a personal safety app. It lets you build a trusted circle of friends, share your live location with them when you choose to, send and answer wellness checks, run safety timers, follow each other's trips, and report unsafe areas. Because safety is the product, some features involve sensitive data — this policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and what we never do with it.

Data we collect

  • Account: email address, display name, username, and optionally a phone number, bio, and profile photo.
  • Location: your position is collected only while location sharing is on, a trip is active, or an alarm is raised — always at your explicit initiative. Background location is used so your circle can still reach you when the app is closed during an active share or trip. You can stop sharing at any time.
  • Safety activity: check-ins, wellness checks and responses, alarms, safety timers, and trips (destination, route, ETA).
  • Messages: chats you send to circle members are stored so they can be delivered and shown in your conversation history.
  • Emergency contacts: names and phone numbers you add, stored to enable one-tap calling and emergency texting. These stay in your account; we never contact them ourselves.
  • Device: a push notification token so alerts can reach your device, and battery level during active trips/shares (so your circle can tell "phone died" from "something is wrong").
  • Community reports: safety reports you submit (location, category, optional note). Reports can be made anonymously.

Who can see your data

Your live location, check-ins, and trip details are visible only to circle members you have accepted — never publicly, never to other users. Safety reports are shown to nearby users, anonymously if you choose. Nothing you do in Artemis is public.

Third-party services

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider. All app data is stored there, encrypted in transit.
  • Expo Push Notifications — delivers push alerts. Receives your push token and notification content.
  • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim / OSRM — when you search for a destination or compute a trip route, the search text and relevant coordinates are sent to these open-source services. No account data is included.
  • Sign in with Google / Apple — if you choose these sign-in methods, we receive your email and name from the provider. We never see your password.

What we never do

  • We never sell your data, to anyone, for any reason.
  • We never show ads or share data with advertisers or data brokers.
  • We never track your location without an active share, trip, or alarm that you started.
  • We never read your messages for any purpose other than delivering them.

Data retention & deletion

Your data is kept while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from Profile → Delete account inside the app — this permanently removes your profile, circle connections, messages, check-ins, trips, and location history. Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

Your rights

Under GDPR (we are based in Sweden) you have the right to access, correct, export, or erase your personal data, and to object to or restrict processing. Use the in-app deletion for erasure, or contact us for anything else.

Children

Artemis is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

If this policy changes materially we will note it in the app and update the effective date above.
