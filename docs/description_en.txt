Artemis is your personal safety circle — a simple way to let the people you trust know you're okay, share your location when it matters, and get help fast if something goes wrong.

Whether you're walking home at night, meeting someone new, on a first date, or just want your family to know you landed safely, Artemis keeps your circle close without being intrusive. Nothing is shared unless you choose to share it.

WELLNESS CHECKS
Send a quick "are you okay?" to anyone in your circle. They get 30 minutes to respond — and can answer right from the lock screen notification, without even opening the app. If they don't respond in time, or if they signal they need help, you're notified immediately.

LIVE TRIP TRACKING
Heading somewhere? Search your destination, choose walking, biking, or driving, and start your trip. Your chosen buddy sees your live route and real-time ETA on a map — updated continuously, even while your phone is locked. If you don't confirm you've arrived safely, they're alerted automatically and can see exactly where you are.

CHECK-IN TIMER
Set a safety timer before a date, a solo hike, or a late shift. If you don't check in before it expires, your circle is alerted and your live location is shared — no need to remember to send anything yourself.

INSTANT ALARM
One tap raises a silent alarm to your entire circle with your live location. In a real emergency, quick access to local emergency numbers is built right in.

YOUR CIRCLE, YOUR RULES
Add trusted friends and family by username — they must accept before you're connected. Choose exactly who sees your location and when. Turn sharing on or off in one tap. Remove anyone, anytime.

MORE WAYS TO STAY SAFE
• Fake incoming call — a believable way to excuse yourself from an uncomfortable situation
• Emergency contacts — one-tap calling or texting for the people who matter most
• Community safety reports — see and report unsafe areas near you, anonymously if you prefer
• Shared calendar — let your circle know your plans and get automatic check-in reminders for events
• Private messaging — stay in touch with your circle directly in the app

YOUR PRIVACY, RESPECTED
Your location is visible only to circle members you've explicitly approved, and only while sharing, a trip, or an alarm is active. We never sell your data, never show ads, and never track you outside the app. Delete your account and all your data permanently, anytime, in one tap.

Artemis works in English and Swedish, and syncs instantly across your circle — because knowing someone has your back shouldn't take effort.

Artemis is a safety companion, not a replacement for emergency services. Always call your local emergency number in a real emergency.
